What Is BeaverPrints?

BeaverPrints is the 47 Beavers' 3D-printing initiative. What started as a way to prototype robot parts for competition has grown into a full program where we design, print, and deliver custom items for our community. Every print supports our team and helps fund our robotics season.

Our team members gain real-world experience in CAD design, additive manufacturing, and project management — all while providing an affordable 3D-printing service to students, families, and local organizations.

How It Works

  1. 1. Submit Your Design

    Send us your 3D model file (STL, OBJ, or 3MF) or describe what you need and our team will design it for you.

  2. 2. We Review & Quote

    Our team reviews your design for printability, suggests material and color options, and provides a fair price quote.

  3. 3. Print & Deliver

    Once approved, we print your item with care and arrange pickup or local delivery in the Montgomery County area.

Materials & Capabilities

We print primarily in PLA and PETG filament, which offer an excellent balance of strength, detail, and affordability. Our printers can produce items up to 250 × 250 × 250 mm with layer resolutions as fine as 0.1 mm.

Multiple colors are available, and we can advise on the best material choice for your project — whether you need flexibility, heat resistance, or a smooth surface finish.
