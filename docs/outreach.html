Our Outreach Mission

The 47 Beavers are committed to giving back to the community that supports us. We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to explore science, technology, engineering, and mathematics — regardless of their background or resources. Our outreach programs are designed to make STEM accessible, exciting, and inclusive.

From hosting workshops at local schools to partnering with international organizations, we work year-round to share our passion for robotics and problem-solving with as many young learners as possible.

Learn More

School & Community Partnerships

We partner with schools throughout Montgomery County — including Poolesville High School, Rocky Hill Middle School, and several elementary schools — to bring robotics demonstrations, coding workshops, and STEM career panels directly to students.

Our team members serve as mentors and role models, showing younger students what is possible when curiosity meets collaboration. These partnerships help build a pipeline of future engineers, scientists, and innovators.

View Partnerships

International Outreach

Our impact extends beyond Montgomery County. The 47 Beavers have collaborated with international organizations to bring STEM education to underserved communities around the world. Through virtual workshops, donated materials, and cross-cultural exchanges, we aim to make robotics a truly global endeavor.

These experiences broaden our own perspectives while empowering young people in other countries to pursue their interest in technology and engineering.
