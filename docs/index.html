Welcome to 47 Beavers Robotics

Founded in 2017, FTC Team 13100 — the 47 Beavers — is a community robotics team made up of students from multiple schools in Montgomery County, Maryland, including Poolesville High School and Rocky Hill Middle School. We are proud to be competing in our eighth season of the FIRST Tech Challenge.

Our team is built on teamwork, growth, STEM outreach, and community involvement. Together we design, build, and program robots while developing the skills and friendships that last a lifetime.

About Us

The 47 Beavers were founded in 2017 as a multi‑school FIRST Tech Challenge team serving students across Montgomery County, Maryland. Our roster draws from Poolesville High School, Rocky Hill Middle School, and neighboring communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote STEM in the community and create meaningful opportunities for younger students to explore science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. We partner with both local and international organizations to expand our outreach impact and inspire the next generation of innovators.

Camps

The 47 Beavers host summer STEM sessions designed to spark curiosity and build hands‑on skills in young learners. Our flagship program, "BeaverCamps," was originally created to help students stay connected and continue learning during and after the COVID‑19 pandemic.

These camps cover topics ranging from basic robotics and coding to creative engineering challenges, giving participants a fun and supportive environment to explore STEM.
