Achievements

Our Journey

Over the years, the 47 Beavers have grown both technically and personally. From our earliest competitions to our most recent season, our team has earned multiple awards that symbolize our continuous progress and development as engineers, collaborators, and community members.

Season Highlights

Each season brings new challenges, new designs, and new opportunities to push ourselves further. Here are some of the moments that define our team's history.

2017 — Founding Season

Our team was founded and entered our first FIRST Tech Challenge competition, laying the groundwork for everything to come.

Growth & Development

Across multiple seasons, we refined our engineering skills, improved our robot designs, and developed stronger team collaboration.

Award-Winning Seasons

Our dedication to gracious professionalism, outreach, and engineering excellence has been recognized with multiple awards at regional events.

Technical Growth

From simple drivetrain designs to complex autonomous routines, our engineering capabilities have evolved dramatically. Our team members gain hands-on experience with CAD software, 3D printing, programming, and mechanical assembly — skills that prepare them for careers in STEM fields.

We also emphasize documentation and the engineering process, ensuring that every design decision is thoughtful, tested, and recorded in our engineering portfolio.

Our Sponsors

We are grateful to our sponsors for their generous support. Their contributions enable us to pursue robotics, attend competitions, and expand our outreach programs across the community.
