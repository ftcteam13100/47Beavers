About the 47 Beavers

Our story, our mission, and our journey through eight seasons of FIRST Tech Challenge.

About Us

The 47 Beavers were founded in 2017 as a multi‑school FIRST Tech Challenge team serving students across Montgomery County, Maryland. Our roster draws from Poolesville High School, Rocky Hill Middle School, and neighboring communities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to promote STEM in the community and create meaningful opportunities for younger students to explore science, technology, engineering, and mathematics. We partner with both local and international organizations to expand our outreach impact and inspire the next generation of innovators.
